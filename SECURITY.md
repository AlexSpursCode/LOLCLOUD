# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

Please report vulnerabilities privately via: aucestovara@gmail.com

Include:

- Affected component/file
- Reproduction details
- Impact and suggested remediation

## Triage SLAs

- Initial acknowledgment: within 3 business days
- Triage decision: within 7 business days
- Remediation target: within 30 days for high/critical issues

Do not disclose vulnerabilities publicly until maintainers confirm a fix or coordinated disclosure timeline.
